No-Nifty a Novartis variant of the Nifty Ten Fifty bike ride



Some Novartis employees and friends are riding the Nifty Ten Fifty route as a personal challenge.

This is an un-sponsored activity. Novartis has no affiliation and liability for this event. You are on your own.
